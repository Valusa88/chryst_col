Crystal Collector

You will be given a "target number" at the start of the game. By clicking on the crystals below, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target score. You lose if your total score goes above the target score.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal. Good Luck!
